Technical Equations

1. Active Power: P = √3 × V × I × Cos(φ) / 1000 [kW]

2. Compensation: Qc = P × [tan(φ1) - tan(φ2)] [kVAR]

3. Capacitance: C = (Qc × 10⁹) / (2π × f × V²) [µF]

4. Avg P.F: Cos(φ) = kWh / √(kWh² + kVARh²)
