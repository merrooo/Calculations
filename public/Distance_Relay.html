🔷 1. Positive Sequence Impedance Z₁ (Ω/km)

📐 Calculator

Result will appear here

📖 Discussion

Positive sequence impedance is the impedance seen by balanced three-phase currents. The reactance \(X_1 = 0.06283 \ln(D_{eq}/GMR)\) Ω/km at 50 Hz depends on conductor geometry.

📐 Equations:
\( Z_1 = R_{ac} + j X_1 \)   where   \( X_1 = 0.06283 \cdot \ln\left(\dfrac{D_{eq}}{GMR}\right) \) Ω/km (50 Hz)

📖 Discussion

Zero sequence impedance includes earth return path. \( R_e = 9.88\times10^{-4}f \) Ω/km, \( D_e = 658.5\sqrt{\rho/f} \) m.

📐 Equations:
\( Z_0 = (R_{ac} + 3R_e) + j \cdot k_{sw} \cdot 0.06283 \ln(D_e / GMR_0) \) with \( GMR_0 = \sqrt[3]{GMR \cdot D^2} \)
